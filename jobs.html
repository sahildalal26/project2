Research Analyst

Reference: RA102 Salary: $78,000 - $90,000 Reports to: Head of Research Services

The Research Analyst will support academic staff by analyzing educational data, preparing reports, and contributing to evidence-based decision making.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Collect and analyze student learning and research data.
  2. Develop reports to support grant applications and projects.
  3. Maintain research databases and ensure data integrity.
  4. Collaborate with teaching staff to identify digital learning trends.

Essential & Preferable Requirements

  • Essential: Bachelor’s degree in Data Science, Statistics, or related field.
  • Essential: Proficiency with data analysis tools (SPSS, Python, or R).
  • Preferable: Experience in higher education research.
  • Preferable: Strong written and presentation skills.

IT Teacher

Reference: IT209 Salary: $72,000 – $85,000 Reports to: Director of Digital Learning

The IT Teacher will deliver high-quality teaching in digital literacy, programming, and IT systems, supporting both undergraduate and postgraduate learning.

Key Responsibilities

  1. Design and deliver engaging IT courses and tutorials.
  2. Support students in research projects and digital tools.
  3. Develop online learning resources using modern platforms.
  4. Contribute to departmental digital learning initiatives.

Essential & Preferable Requirements

  • Essential: Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology or Computer Science.
  • Essential: Teaching qualification or proven teaching experience.
  • Preferable: Knowledge of learning management systems (LMS).
  • Preferable: Experience with web technologies (HTML, CSS, JavaScript).
